#Aldub

The most efficient utilization of social media marketing


Who would have thought that an unlikely couple would emerge from the far east and break all sorts of social media records and turn themselves into bonafide mainstream media personalities. They bypassed the typical brand x propaganda which insinuated that only 720 machines were needed to produce 5 million tweets within 24 hours. If that is really the case then logically we would only need 4000 to 5000 machines to emulate the 35 million tweets which was set by FIFA World Cup. If only twitter will allow each machine to tweet with unlimited power that can possibly happen but brand x failed to mention that there is a tweet limit for each of the 720 accounts and I am not talking about the character limit. By attempting to invalidate Aldub's non-conventional popularity train the brand x invalidated Super Bowl and FIFA as well. Brand x was always sticking to manufacturing popularity that they forgot what charisma means. The sheer magnitude of animal magnetism emanating from Alden and Yaya Dub might definitely be the case, the hard work they put in to continuously improve as entertainers might also be the greatest factor but my take is as humble as their beginnings if one can exploit the power of social media marketing, one will cut down the usual high cost of promoting oneself in the mainstream media outmaneuver the traps and overhead cost of maintaining your status in show business industry. In short annihilate the influence of powers-that-be or shall we call them the merchants of cool. As unsure as they are when they were just starting their rookie bullrun on boobtube we will never know if there really is an end to the phenomenon that their fans and bashers call as Aldub Fever.
